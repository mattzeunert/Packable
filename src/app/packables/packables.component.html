Packables

Packables are "smart" items you take with you on a trip.

Each Packable has its own Quantity Rules (eg. 1 Per day ) and Weather Conditions (eg. only for rainy weather ), to help the app calculate how many and when to pack them.

You can import some of the Packables we prepared for you, or if you can't find what you want simply create your own!

When added to Collections and Travelers, a Packble can have different rules applied to it to fit the specific Collection and Traveler.

To make changes to the rules of a Packable across all your Collections and Travelers make sure you are editing them through the Packables page.
