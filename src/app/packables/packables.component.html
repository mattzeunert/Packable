Packables

Packables are items you can pack. For example, a T-Shirt, or a Toothbrush.

You can either download prepared Packables, or create them from scratch, then you add them to your profiles

You can set the Packables' default packing conditions, like how many Shirts you want to pack per day of your trip.

Once Packables are in a Profile, their settings can be customised to the individual's needs! This will not change the default settings of the Packable or the settings in any other Profile.
