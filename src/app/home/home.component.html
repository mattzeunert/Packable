Packable.app

Packable.app allows you to quickly create a packing list for your upcoming trips.

You can use pre-defined templates to create a Traveler profile, and later customize your preferences so that next time you travel you can simple rely on Packable.app to calculate and tell you exactly what you need to pack.

Packables

Packables are "smart" items you take with you on a trip.

Each Packable has its own Quantity Rules (eg. 1 Per day ) and Weather Conditions (eg. only for rainy weather ), to help the app calculate how many and when to pack them.

Collections

Collections are groups of Packables that share the same category (e.g. Toiletries).

You can pick the Collections for every trip based on the activities planned, like a Beach Collection, while others can be set as Essential Collections, and will automatically be considered for every trip, like a Basic Clothes Collection.

Each Collections can have its own Weather Conditions to limit when the app would suggest you should pack its contained Packables.

Travelers

Traveler profiles help you group Collections with custom preferences, so that the app could know what you always need to pack, and remember your individual preferences for each Traveler profile.

You can define multiple Traveler profiles for various types of travel, like one for a business trip and another for holidays, each with different rules for their Collections.

Going away with your family or friends? No problem, you can add multiple Traveler profiles to a single trip, each with their own preferences and it would help you keep track of Shared Packables too so you won't pack sunscreen twice!
