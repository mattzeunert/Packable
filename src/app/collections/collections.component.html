Collections

Collections are groups of Packables that share the same category (eg. Toiletries) or relate to a certain activity (eg. Swimming).

You can pick the Collections for every trip based on the activities planned, like a Beach Collection , while others can be set as Essential Collections, and will automatically be considered for every trip, like a Basic Clothes Collection .

Each Collections can have its own Weather Conditions to limit when the app would suggest you should pack its contained Packables.

You can import Collections we've set up for you, or simply make your own.

You can customise your Collections and import them to your Travelers, then customize them further for every Traveler.

While you may want to pack your shaving razor with your toiletries, your 9 year old kid could probably do without one!

You can set conditions on the Collection and it's Packables, and only if those conditions are met then the Packables will be added to a trip.

E.g You can set a weather condition to only pack a Jumper, a Sweater and a Rain Jacker if the temperature is Below 15 Degrees during a certain trip

Essential Collections

Marking a collection as Essential means it will always be automatically selected when you set a trip. This is perticularly useful for basic needs like clothes and toiletries.

You can still set weather conditions on Essential Collections, and the Packing List calculator would take that into account.
