    Profiles

    Profiles can be created for each person you may want to pack for.

    A Profile may contain loose Packables, or Collections with Packables.

    You can also customise the quantities and conditions for each Packable or Collection you add, without affecting other profiles that use the same Packables.

    Once you finished creating a Profile you can add it to a Trip and create a packing list per your settings.

    You should always tweak and change your profile after each trip to optimise it's settings until you have the perfect set up for any trip!

    You may download basic Profiles, with common Packables to get you started.
