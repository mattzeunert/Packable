Travelers

Traveler profiles help you group Collections with custom preferences, so that the app could know what you always need to pack, and remember your individual preferences for each Traveler profile.

We've set up a few templates to choose from, but once you get started you can add or remove collections and packables to make that Traveler suit your needs.

Changes you make to Collections and Packables while editing a Traveler will not change other Traveler profiles or even the default Packable and Collection rules.

You can define multiple Traveler profiles for various types of travel, like one for a business trip and another for holidays, each with different rules for their Collections.

Going away with your family or friends? No problem, you can add multiple Traveler profiles to a single trip, each with their own preferences and it would help you keep track of Shared Packables too so you won't pack sunscreen twice!
